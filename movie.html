La La Land

2016

Romance

Music

Drama

"La La Land" is a dazzling ode to the golden age of Hollywood, blending romance, music, and dance into a vibrant tapestry of dreams and reality. Directed by Damien Chazelle, this modern classic captures the bittersweet journey of two aspiring artists as they navigate love and ambition in the city of stars.

Detailed Plot Summary

Mia, an aspiring actress, serves lattes to movie stars in between auditions, and Sebastian, a jazz musician, scrapes by playing cocktail party gigs in dingy bars. As success mounts, they are faced with decisions that begin to fray the fragile fabric of their love affair, and the dreams they worked so hard to maintain in each other threaten to rip them apart.

Director's Vision

Director Damien Chazelle drew inspiration from classic Hollywood musicals and aimed to create a modern tribute to the genre. His vision was to blend the charm and style of old Hollywood with contemporary storytelling.

Awards and Nominations

  • Winner of 6 Academy Awards including Best Director and Best Actress
  • Received 14 Academy Award nominations
  • Winner of 7 Golden Globe Awards

Critical Reception

"La La Land" received widespread critical acclaim, with praise for its direction, performances, screenplay, and musical numbers. Rotten Tomatoes gives it a 91% approval rating, and Metacritic has a score of 93 out of 100.

Soundtrack and Music

The movie's soundtrack features original songs that blend jazz and contemporary musical styles. Notable tracks include "City of Stars" and "Another Day of Sun."

Trivia and Fun Facts

  • Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling took intensive dance and singing lessons to prepare for their roles.
  • The movie was shot in over 60 locations around Los Angeles.
  • Director Damien Chazelle wrote the script in 2010 but couldn't get it made until after the success of "Whiplash" in 2014.

Memorable Quotes

"Here's to the ones who dream, foolish as they may seem." - Mia
"You could just write your own rules. You know, write something that's as interesting as you are." - Sebastian
